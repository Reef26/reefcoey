Leckerbissen is a newsletter organised, written, edited, and published by students of the German Department of School of Modern Lanuages (and sometimes students of other schools) at Cardiff University. I made the website and still maintain it. The size of the team varies from issue to issue, I did majority of the design and editing of the Spring 2025 issue and have also contributed a few of my own articles throughout a few of the editions.

Running this is very much a small hobby, I don't really have plans other running a professionally presented game server and getting to play some Minecraft with friends.
